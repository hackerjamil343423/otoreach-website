**Primary Palette**

- **Electric Lime Green:** This is the main accent color used for Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons (like "Apply Now," "Get the Framework"), highlight tags, and graphical elements.
    - *Approximate Hex:* `#D4F657` or `#D1F54B`
- **Off-White / Cream:** The main background color of the website is not pure white; it is a very light, warm cream or "bone" color, which gives it a premium feel.
    - *Approximate Hex:* `#FAF9F6` or `#FDFBF7`
- **Rich Black / Dark Charcoal:** Used for the footer background, the main headlines, and primary text. It contrasts sharply with the lime green.
    - *Approximate Hex:* `#111111` or `#0F0F0F`

**Secondary / Neutral Palette**

- **Medium Gray:** Used for body copy, subheadlines, and descriptions to soften the reading experience compared to pure black.
    - *Approximate Hex:* `#555555`
- **Light Gray (Borders/Cards):** Used subtly for the borders of the content cards and sections.
    - *Approximate Hex:* `#E5E5E5`

**Summary of Usage:**
The brand relies heavily on a **high-contrast aesthetic**, using the **Electric Lime** against **Black** and **Cream** to direct attention to conversion points (buttons) and key information (tags).
