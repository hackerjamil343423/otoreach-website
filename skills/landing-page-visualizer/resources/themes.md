# Visual Design Themes for Landing Pages

Use these themes to ensure consistency across all generated assets. When suggesting themes to the user, pick the ones that best fit their `brand.md` or general vibe.

## 1. 3D Glassmorphism & Claymation
**Keywords:** 3D illustration, soft clay render, pastel colors, translucent glass UI elements, frosted glass, highly detailed, octane render, soft studio lighting, playful, modern.
**Best for:** SaaS platforms, modern tech startups, playful consumer apps, and modern "B2C" dashboards.

## 2. Flat Vector Minimalist
**Keywords:** flat vector illustration, minimal, solid shapes, geometric, corporate memphis style, clean lines, vibrant flat colors, transparent background style, dribbble style.
**Best for:** B2B software, corporate websites, clean and minimal developer tools.

## 3. Neo-Brutalism
**Keywords:** neo-brutalism, bold high contrast colors, thick black outlines, retro interface, offset drop shadows, isometric perspective, raw, edgy graphic design.
**Best for:** Creative agencies, developer tools, trendy modern brands aiming for a "Gen-Z" or quirky aesthetic.

## 4. Ethereal & Abstract Gradients (Backgrounds)
**Keywords:** fluid gradient waves, glowing ethereal light, holographic, soft bokeh, abstract mesh gradient, clean empty negative space, ultra high resolution, UI layout background.
**Best for:** AI landing pages, Web3, luxury tech, hero sections.

## 5. Clean Isometric
**Keywords:** 3D isometric illustration, orthographic view, miniature diorama, clean lighting, white background, detailed, vector art style.
**Best for:** Logistics, analytics dashboards, fintech, showing complex systems simply.

## 6. Minimal Line Art (Monoline)
**Keywords:** continuous single line art, minimal UI icon style, clean linework, subtle pastel accent color, lots of negative space.
**Best for:** High-end elegant brands, luxury, architecture, minimal wellness sites.

## 7. Flat Layered 3D Device Mockup (OtoReach Brand Style)
**Keywords:** 3D flat layered illustration, device mockup, rounded tablet, smartphone, lime-green screen, dark black device, subtle drop shadows, glossy surfaces, modern digital mockup, clean UI elements on screen, pure white background, polished, professional, SaaS card illustration.
**Color Palette:** Primary: bright lime green (#D4F657), Secondary: deep black (#1A1A1A), Background: pure white (#FFFFFF), Accent: white text on dark, dark text on light.
**Style Details:**
- Devices with large rounded corners (20-30px radius)
- Layered/stacked composition — devices overlap slightly for depth
- Soft diffuse drop shadows beneath each device
- Flat UI elements on device screens (charts, icons, chat bubbles, buttons)
- Smooth glossy screen surfaces
- Even, consistent lighting — no harsh shadows
- Background MUST be pure solid white, no gradients or noise
**Best for:** SaaS service cards, product feature illustrations, tech startup service sections, AI/automation product showcases.
**Prompt Template:** `A 3D flat layered illustration of [subject] on a lime-green rounded tablet. Show [UI details] on screen. A dark black phone overlaps beneath showing [secondary detail]. All devices have rounded corners, smooth glossy surfaces, and subtle soft drop shadows. Bright lime green (#D4F657) and black (#1A1A1A) color palette with pure white background. The background MUST be pure solid white with no gradients or noise. Clean, modern, polished digital mockup style. Square 1:1.`

## 8. 3D Glossy Emotional Objects (Pain Point Style)
**Keywords:** 3D glossy render, isolated emotional object, symbolic metaphor, floating accent elements, lime green and black color palette, soft diffuse drop shadows, polished surfaces, pure white background, conceptual illustration, pain point visual.
**Color Palette:** Primary: bright lime green (#D4F657), Secondary: deep black (#1A1A1A), Background: pure white (#FFFFFF), Accent: red for warnings/alerts.
**Style Details:**
- One central 3D glossy object that symbolizes the pain point (hourglass for time loss, low-battery figure for burnout, shrinking coins for cost, alarm bell for stress)
- 3-5 smaller floating accent objects orbiting the main object to reinforce the concept (chat bubbles, calendars, clocks, exclamation marks)
- All objects share the lime-green and black color palette
- Smooth, polished, glossy surfaces with soft studio lighting
- Subtle drop shadow beneath the main object for depth
- Background MUST be pure solid white — no gradients, noise, or floors
- Emotional and immediately recognizable — the viewer should "feel" the problem
**Best for:** Problem sections, pain point cards, "why you need this" sections, fear/urgency messaging, customer frustration illustrations.
**Prompt Template:** `A 3D glossy rendered [main object] symbolizing [pain point concept]. The [main object] is lime green (#D4F657) with dark black (#1A1A1A) [details]. Small floating 3D [accent elements] surround it. Smooth glossy surfaces, soft diffuse drop shadows. The background MUST be pure solid white with absolutely no gradients or noise. Clean, modern, polished 3D render style. Square 1:1.`
**Proven examples:**
- Hourglass with floating chat bubbles → "late reply = lost customer"
- Exhausted humanoid figure with low-battery icon, clock, calendar → "employee limits"
- Shrinking coin stacks with leaking money bag → "costs eating profits"
- Alarm bell with 99+ notification badges and exclamation marks → "constant worry"

## 9. 3D Glossy Tool & Service Icons (Feature/Value Style)
**Keywords:** 3D glossy render, isolated professional tool, floating supporting elements, lime green and black color palette, soft diffuse drop shadows, polished surfaces, pure white background, service illustration, feature card visual.
**Color Palette:** Primary: bright lime green (#D4F657), Secondary: deep black (#1A1A1A), Background: pure white (#FFFFFF).
**Style Details:**
- One central 3D glossy professional tool or device that represents the service/feature (magnifying glass for research, pen for copywriting, laptop for design, rocket for speed)
- 3-5 smaller floating elements that add context (data charts, text blocks, wireframes, lightning bolts)
- All objects share the lime-green and black color palette
- Smooth, polished, glossy surfaces with soft studio lighting
- Subtle drop shadow beneath the main object for depth
- Background MUST be pure solid white — no gradients, noise, or floors
- Professional and aspirational — the viewer should feel the value/expertise
**Best for:** Feature cards, service descriptions, "what we offer" sections, value propositions, process/methodology steps.
**Prompt Template:** `A 3D glossy rendered [tool/device] representing [service/feature]. The [tool/device] is [lime green (#D4F657) / dark black (#1A1A1A)] with [contrasting color] [details]. Small floating 3D [supporting elements] surround it. Smooth glossy surfaces, soft diffuse drop shadows. The background MUST be pure solid white with absolutely no gradients or noise. Clean, modern, polished 3D render style. Square 1:1.`
**Proven examples:**
- Magnifying glass leaning against rising bar chart → "Research & Analysis"
- Fountain pen with glowing ink trail on floating document → "Content / Copywriting"
- Laptop/tablet showing wireframe layout with design elements → "Design"
- Rocket launching with stopwatch and lightning bolts → "Speed / Fast Delivery"
